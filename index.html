About us

We are Verduritas, a new startup focused on upgrading and innovating the field of gastronomy with new technologies. We want to provide a trustworthy interaction with our clients for the sake of a great experience.

About the product

Transform the shape of your lunch hour with an explosion of flavors at the click of a button. We provide a wide range of restaurants available for all tastes. GastroGo offers you a digital and intuitive solution to make ordering quick and easy.

Features

For workers
  • Order your food according to what your team wants!
  • Get the important information of the restaurant that caught your attention.
  • Don’t worry about dividing the bill! GastroGo will do it for you.
  • Need to remove some ingredients because of allergies? You can remove them in just a click!
  • Get the latest recommendations according to your previous orders.
Try now!

For restaurants
  • GastroGo will show you all the orders in order, to avoid confusions.
  • Too many orders already in the kitchen? GastroGo allows you to reject orders after checking what they include!
  • We provide you a tracker for each order. If it gets cancelled or changed, you will be notified asap!
Try now!

How it works?

Select the restaurant and what menu would your team want

Personalize your order according to your taste

Confirm your order and send it!

The delivery will go immediately to you. Enjoy!
